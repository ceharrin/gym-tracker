# GymTracker Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 5, 2026

## Overview

GymTracker is a workout tracking app that helps you log workouts, body measurements, and custom activities.

This Privacy Policy explains what information GymTracker stores and how it is used.

## Information We Collect

GymTracker may collect and store the following information that you choose to add in the app:

- Profile information, such as your name
- Profile photo
- Workout data, including workout titles, exercises, sets, repetitions, weight, distance, duration, laps, notes, and personal-record flags
- Body measurement data, including weight, body fat percentage, and related notes
- Custom activities and exercise instructions you create or edit

## How We Use Your Information

GymTracker uses this information only to provide the core functionality of the app, including:

- Saving your workouts and workout history
- Showing progress, trends, and personal records
- Saving your profile and body measurements
- Storing your data locally on your device
- Exporting or printing workout data when you request it

GymTracker does not use your information for advertising or marketing.

## Local Storage and Backups

GymTracker stores your app data locally on your device.

If you choose to export a backup, workout file, or printed/exported report, the exported file is created only when you request it and is shared only through the system share sheet or print flow that you choose.

## Data Not Used for Tracking

GymTracker does not use your data to track you across apps, websites, or services operated by other companies.

GymTracker does not use third-party advertising SDKs.

## Photos

If you choose a profile photo, the selected image is stored for your use in the app and may sync through iCloud when sync is enabled.

GymTracker only accesses photos that you explicitly choose through the system photo picker.

## Data Sharing

GymTracker does not sell your personal information.

GymTracker does not share your data with third-party advertisers or data brokers.

Your data may be shared only in these limited cases:

- When you explicitly choose to export, print, or share workout data
- When required by law

## Data Storage and Retention

Your data is stored locally on your device.

If you delete the app, local data may be removed from your device unless you have exported a backup beforehand.

Data remains available until you delete it from the app or remove the app from your device.

## Your Choices

You can:

- Edit or delete workouts, body measurements, and custom activities in the app
- Choose whether to add a profile photo
- Export or share data only when you explicitly request it
- Export a local backup before deleting the app or moving to a new device

## Children’s Privacy

GymTracker is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the “Last updated” date above.
